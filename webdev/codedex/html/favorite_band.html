Nightwish

Nightwish is a Finnish symphonic metal band formed in 1996 by keyboardist Tuomas Holopainen, guitarist Emppu Vuorinen, and lead singer Tarja Turunen.
Known for their powerful blend of metal and orchestral music, Nightwish has become one of the most successful bands in their genre.
They are celebrated for their dynamic compositions, intricate musical arrangements, and the distinct, operatic vocals of their singers,
including current vocalist Floor Jansen. Nightwish's music often explores themes of fantasy, mythology, and nature, with notable albums like Once, Dark Passion Play,
and Endless Forms Most Beautiful showcasing their evolving sound and artistic vision.
